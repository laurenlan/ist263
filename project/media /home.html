Banksy. One of the most well recognized artists in modern day. Their art has been pictured all over
the world and oftentimes brings a frenzy of Banksy lovers like myself
to the location within a few minutes of it being posted to social media or on another platform.
But who is this artist? Well your guess is as good as mine! One of the greatest mysteries in the current art world is who
is this artist with 11.2 million followers on instagram and following no one, creating almost
parody-like art about the world, and the fifth best selling artist in the world. While they have traveled the world, made books, and even a movie titled The Exit Through the Gift Shop, the only thing
people can confidently say is that the artist is from the Bristol area of the United Kingdom.
