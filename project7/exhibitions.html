Currently you can view a Banksy collection at the Palace of Fine Arts in San Francisco until February 27th, 2022.
Prices range from forty to fifty dollars and availability is limited as this is a private show, however it is on tour throughout the country.

>

Girl with Balloon sold in 2018 for a crazy $1,135,219 even though no one estimated it would sell for that high.

>

While the best way to see Banksy's art is on the street there are exhibitions all over the world which house some of their prints and wall graffiti pieces.

>
>

If you want to see an original piece however, I highly recommend following Banksy on Instagram as they are often posting their new pieces on their account.
Searching the web for locations where the art is still up is also a great way to find their art!
